\doxysubsubsection{Detailed Description}
Wall clock timer. 

A \mbox{\hyperlink{classUtil_1_1Timer}{Timer}} keeps track of the time elapsed during one or more interval. Each interval begins when \mbox{\hyperlink{classUtil_1_1Timer_a764719f9d15f7f600cd321642b327b2b}{start()}} is called and ends when \mbox{\hyperlink{classUtil_1_1Timer_a462dc6312e69bbf03e169fff832bd562}{stop()}} is called. If \mbox{\hyperlink{classUtil_1_1Timer_a764719f9d15f7f600cd321642b327b2b}{start()}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{classUtil_1_1Timer_a462dc6312e69bbf03e169fff832bd562}{stop()}} are invoked repeatedly, the timer accumulates the time elapses in multiple intervals. The accumulated time is returned by the \mbox{\hyperlink{classUtil_1_1Timer_ac411a514744abf68efc18532388c8b0b}{time()}} method, and can be reset to zero by the \mbox{\hyperlink{classUtil_1_1Timer_a7759ffbb6a675d82f67edcdf1646685c}{clear()}} method.
